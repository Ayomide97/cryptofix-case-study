CryptoFix — UX Case Study

Complete Website Redesign for a Crypto Ad Network

UX/UI Designer
Figma, Notion, Whimsical
End-to-end redesign

1. Project Overview

CryptoFix is an ad agency operating in the cryptocurrency space. It connects advertisers with high-traffic crypto platforms—such as exchanges, blogs, and influencers—to run targeted ad campaigns. I was tasked with redesigning their website from the ground up to reflect their authority, simplify the advertiser onboarding process, and promote their analytics and partnership strengths.

Client

CryptoFix

Role

UX/UI Designer

Scope

End-to-end website redesign (strategy, IA, UX, UI)

Tools

Figma, Notion, Whimsical

2. The Problem / Client Brief

CryptoFix's existing website did not reflect the quality or professionalism of their offering. They requested a complete redesign with no reference to their current site. The goal was to create a clean, professional experience that clearly communicates their value to advertisers and publishers in the crypto space.

Primary goals from the client brief:

  • Professional & serious tone
  • Highlight partnerships with the top 5 crypto exchanges
  • Emphasize a comprehensive analytics system
  • Include all ad types and partners
  • Avoid using pinks, browns, comic fonts, or serif fonts
  • Make it fully responsive for all screen sizes

3. Business Goals & User Needs

Business Objectives

  • Communicate CryptoFix's credibility and reach
  • Help advertisers easily create campaigns and understand ad options
  • Allow crypto platforms (publishers) to list ad types and attract advertisers
  • Position CryptoFix ahead of competitors like RichPush, CryptoToads, and CoinTraffic

User Needs

There are two user types:

Advertisers

  • Want clear ad package info
  • Need trustworthy data/analytics
  • Prefer flexible payment (Crypto & USD)
  • Value ease of use when setting up campaigns

Publishers (Crypto websites, influencers)

  • Want visibility and trustworthiness when listing their ad space
  • Need a professional partner that brings consistent, high-converting traffic

4. Discovery & Strategy

Audience

  • Male, aged 25–34
  • Global audience
  • Crypto-savvy, ROI-focused, expects data-driven platforms

Key Features Identified

  • Partnered with top 5 exchanges
  • NFT-based ad minting option
  • Deep analytics dashboard
  • Campaign creation form (with budget slider & refund policy)
  • Responsive login and contact pages
  • Testimonial + client logos for trust

6. Visual Language & UI Design

The visual direction aimed for a serious, tech-forward aesthetic:

Color

Neutral scheme (cool grays, dark blue, or teal tones), avoiding pinks and browns

Typography

Clean, modern sans-serif typefaces

Layout

Modular design with generous white space for clarity

Icons & Illustrations

Crypto-themed yet minimalistic

Tone

Straightforward, no fluff, data-focused

7. High-Fidelity Design & Prototype

Each page was designed to prioritize hierarchy and clarity. Key elements included:

  • Home hero section that immediately communicates value
  • Cards showing ad types (banner sizes, influencers, video platforms)
  • Campaign creation form with intuitive UI and trust statements
  • A section showcasing exchange partnerships and client logos
  • Contact form with embedded social links

9. Reflection & Learnings

This project sharpened my ability to:

  • Design from a clean slate with minimal visual guidance
  • Build clear UX flows for two separate user types (advertisers & publishers)
  • Align visual design with brand voice in a rapidly evolving industry
  • Balance crypto-native aesthetics with accessibility and clarity

If I had more time, I'd explore:

  • User onboarding flows or onboarding animations
  • Building a live MVP version with a CMS or Webflow
  • A dashboard concept for advertisers to track campaign performance
